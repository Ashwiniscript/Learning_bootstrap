Hockey

Hockey is a family of sports in which two teams play against each other, using sticks to propel a ball or puck into a goal. There are many variations of hockey, including field hockey, ice hockey, roller hockey, and street hockey. The most common forms involve two teams of players trying to score by hitting a ball or puck into the opposing team's goal.
