Welcome To Bootstrap

Bootstrap has multiple meanings, generally relating to self-reliance and starting from a minimal base. In its most literal sense, a bootstrap is a loop on a boot that aids in pulling it on. Figuratively, it means improving one's situation or starting something (like a business) with minimal resources, relying primarily on one's own efforts and ingenuity. In computing, "bootstrap" refers to loading a minimal program to start a larger system, often the operating system.
